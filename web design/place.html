Toonzy Airline

Linda Loo lives in a tall old building in Looville, she has a small apartment. Looville was founded in the early cinema era, around the 1950's. However since Linda Loo is a stewardess in a big airline company called Toony airline she does spend most of her time working on a plane. Shes never really in one place too long. She's in charge of controlling the toons that travel using Toonzy Airline.
