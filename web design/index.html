Linda Loo the Stewardess

Shes an employe for an airline company called Toonzy Airline in a Looville. She lives in a regular studio aparment in New York. However since shes a stewardess she travels through the whole world and has a chance to meet different toons from differet era, as they board to get to their films. As a stewardess shes in charge of making sure the flight goes smoothly as possible, But is that really possible if toons are on board. Thats where Linda Loo comes in, shes in charge of rounding up the toons and make sure theyre as relaxed as possible, so they don't cause a mess of the plane.
