Toonzy's Airline shift

Linda Loo works for Toonzy Airline, a big company that focuses on boarding different toons from different eras. They make sure that although most toons are destructive and full of anxiety, Toonzy Airline with the help of Linda Loo, help in soothing the toons so they can travel in piece without hurting themselves or each other.

Project Statement


For this project I tried to use inspiration from old cartoons and animations from a character called Peacock from Skull girlts. I thought making the circle move was cute, since the Peacock has something similar. I made it my own by adding different animations and making it slightly funny and cute as the little ball character moves and bounces off and it continues as a loop, i really like how it came out. Since it was frame by frame I ended up with a lot of frames, roughly 400-500. I did use tweening so it'd be easier for me to animate, and I did learn to animate in photoshop a bit which was definitely really fun. For my storyboard my idea was just have her chase another toon, but it proved to be too big a challenge, same as adding the audio, i had a hard time finding sounds that reminded me of the toons. But i think that's the closest i got, i didn want to incorporate the jingle that the airlines use when they need to make an announcement . But that sound was the closest I got to what I wanted.
